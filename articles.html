AI Integration and Advanced Speech Recognition

One trend shaping interpretation is the integration of artificial intelligence, which leverages machine learning to analyze and interpret speech more effectively. These systems have improved in accuracy and are increasingly adaptable across multiple languages, making them useful in diverse contexts, from live interpreting to automatic transcription. Notably, automated speech recognition is reducing the cognitive load on interpreters by providing preliminary translations that professionals can quickly refine (IEEE Xplore, 2024).
