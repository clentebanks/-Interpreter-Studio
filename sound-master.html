How to Use

  1. Install InterpreterSound Master from the Chrome Web Store or Microsoft Edge Add-ons.
  2. Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar to open the control panel.
  3. Use the volume slider to adjust the sound level of the current tab, or select a different tab from the list to manage its audio.
  4. Utilize the boost feature to amplify audio up to 800%, ensuring clear and loud sound output.
